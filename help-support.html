Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ride?

To book a ride, open the SwiftRide app and tap "Book Ride". Enter your pickup location and destination, select your preferred ride type, and confirm your booking. You'll see driver details and can track your ride in real-time.

How do I order food?

Tap "Order Food" from the main menu. Browse restaurants, select your items, add them to cart, and proceed to checkout. You can track your order and communicate with the restaurant through the app.

How do I add money to my wallet?

Go to the Wallet section, tap "Add Money", enter the amount you want to add, and select your payment method. The money will be instantly credited to your SwiftRide wallet for easy payments.

How do I earn reward points?

You earn points for every ride and food order. Complete rides, leave reviews, refer friends, and participate in special promotions to earn bonus points. Check the Rewards section for your current balance and available rewards.

How do I apply a promo code?

Go to Promo Codes section, enter your promo code, and tap "Add Code". The code will be saved to your account and automatically applied to eligible orders during checkout.

What should I do if my order is delayed?

If your order is running late, you can track it in real-time. Contact the driver/restaurant through the app, or reach out to our support team for assistance. We may offer compensation for significant delays.

How do I update my payment method?

Go to Settings > Payment Settings > Default Payment Method. You can add new cards, update existing ones, or change your default payment option from the Wallet section as well.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your payment information. We never store full card details on our servers and comply with PCI DSS standards.
