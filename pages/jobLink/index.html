Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Simply click the "Get Started" or "Login" button, fill out the registration form with your details, and verify your email address. It takes less than 2 minutes!

Is CareerBridge free to use?

Yes! Creating an account and browsing opportunities is completely free for job seekers, freelancers, and students. We also offer premium features for enhanced visibility.

How are job listings verified?

All employers go through a verification process. We review company details, contact information, and cross-check listings to ensure authenticity and protect our users from scams.

What training resources are available?

We provide resume building tools, interview preparation guides, skill development courses, and career coaching resources to help you succeed in your job search.

Can I apply for multiple positions?

Absolutely! You can apply to as many positions as you like. We recommend tailoring your application for each role to increase your chances of success.

How do I contact support?
